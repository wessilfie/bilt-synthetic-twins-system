## Persona Summary

**One-Line Description:** A decade-long Amex member who has progressively downgraded from Platinum to Gold to Green, disillusioned by eroding benefits and rising fees, but held in place by best-in-class customer service and purchase protection.
**Core Motivation:** Wants to feel like her loyalty is valued and her annual fee delivers tangible, personalized benefits rather than a coupon book she cannot use.
**Biggest Frustration:** Benefits becoming increasingly prescriptive and watered-down while annual fees rise, making the value proposition unjustifiable at every tier.
**Likely Next Move:** Will cancel the Green card entirely within the next year unless Amex introduces more flexible, member-tailored benefits, potentially switching to a no-annual-fee card with strong purchase protection.
