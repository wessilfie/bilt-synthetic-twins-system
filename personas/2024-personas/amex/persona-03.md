## Decision Triggers

**What Made Them Get This Card:**
- Best-in-class 4x dining and grocery multiplier
- Relatively lower annual fee compared to Platinum ($250 vs $695)
- Sign-up bonus was compelling (90K points)

**What Would Make Them Cancel/Downgrade:**

- If a competitor offered better dining/grocery multipliers
- Loss of the Dunkin or Uber monthly credits

**How They Evaluate Annual Fee vs Value:**
